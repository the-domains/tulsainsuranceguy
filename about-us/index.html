About Us

At Tulsa Insurance Guy we believe in service and communication. Our owners Jimmy and Rebecca Eubanks are committed to finding the best possible coverage for you and your family. The company was founded in 2008 inTulsa, Oklahoma and is currently located in the heart of downtown Tulsa. Jimmy began his career at Liberty Mutual Group and quickly realized how limited he was by only being able to provide one company. While at Liberty Mutual, Jimmy was ranked the number one agent in Tulsa and ranked number three out of five states. Now Jimmy is partnered with theOAA and on average we are able to save our clients 28% off of their current insurance premiums. With over 81 companies to choose from, we are “Your Local Insurance Solution”.
